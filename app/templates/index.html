About Us

This project was developed by the UC Davis Computational Linguistics Lab.

Our Algorithm

We used OpenFST to build a finite state transducer which generates lexically and syntactically diverse paraphrases for sentences and scores them based on similarity to training utterances. Our application can score language models based on perplexity, create new training data based on paraphrases of user-entered utterances, retrain the model, and demonstrate the change in perplexity.
